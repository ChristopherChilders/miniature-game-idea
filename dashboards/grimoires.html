Grimoire Construction

A Players Grimoire is composed of 40 cards. They can have no more can 3 copies of any given card, including variant cards.

List of Basic Cards

Magic Weapons

Can be used by any Character

  • Giant Blade: +3 Strength
  • Sword of Bloodshed: +3 Attacks
  • Obsidian Blade: No Armor Saves
  • Ogre Blade: +2 Strength
  • Sword of Strife: +2 Attacks
  • Fencer's Blade: AHW & WS 10
  • Sword of Anti-Heroes: +1 Attack and Strength for every enemy Character in base contact with bearer or bearer's unit.
  • Spellthieving Sword: If a Wizard take a wound, they become Deadlocked
  • Sword of Swift Slaying: ASF
  • Sword of Battle: +1 Attack
  • Berserker Sword: Frenzy & can never lose it
  • Sword of Might: +1 Strength
  • Gold Sigil Sword: ???
  • Sword of Striking: +1 to Hit
  • Biting Blade: Armor Piercing
  • Relic Sword: Always Wounds on a 5+ or better
  • Shrieking Blade: Fear
  • Tormentor Sword: Monster or Character wounded gains Stupidity for rest of game
  • Warrior Bane: Monster or Character wounded -1 Attack for each wound, permanently
Magic Armor

Can be used by non-Wizard Characters unless otherwise stated

  • Armor of Destiny: Heavy Armor & 4+ ward
  • Trickster's Helm: Save +1 & successful Wounds have to be rerolled
  • Armor of Silvered Steel: Save = 2+
  • Armor of Fortune: Heavy Armor & 5+ ward
  • Helm of Discord: Armor +1 & Characters in base contact have to take Ld test. Failed = ASL
  • Glittering Scales: Light Armor. -1 to hit
  • Shield of Ptolos: Shield. +1 Armor against shooting
  • Spellshield: Shield. Magic Resistance (1)
  • Gambler's Armor: Heavy Armor. 6+ Ward
  • Dragonhelm: +1 Armor & +2 Ward vs fire
  • Enchanted Shield: Shield. Armor +2
  • Charmed Shield: Shield. One Use Only. First Wound taken has a 2+ ward

Enchanted Items

Can Target any Character.

  • Wizarding Hat: become a level 2 Wizard of a basic Lore of your choice, but has Stupidity
  • Fozzrik's Folding Fortress: Set up a Building you own but isn't on the battlefield
  • Arabyan Carpet: Target Infantry or Monsterous Infantry Character gains Fly
  • Crown of Command: Target Character gains Stubborn
  • Healing Potion: One Use Only. Target Character regain d6 lost Wounds
  • Featherfoe Torc: Units with the Fly keyword reroll successful to hit rolls against the bearer and their unit
  • Ruby Ring of Ruin: Bound Spell (power level 3). Fireball (lv 1)
  • The Terrifying Mask of EEE!: Terror, but can't use Inspiring Presence.
  • Potion of Strength: One Use Only. +3 Strength until end of turn.
  • Potion of Toughness: One Use Only. +3 Toughness until end of turn.
  • The Other Trickster's Shard: Models in Base to Base contact with the bearer reroll successful Ward saves
  • Ironcurse Icon: 6+ for bearer and unit against War Machines
  • Potion of Foolhardiness: One Use Only. At Start of Turn, target Character & unit gain Immune to Psychology and Devastating Charge until end of turn.
  • Potion of Speed: One Use Only. Target Character gaines Always Strikes First until end of turn.
